Exhibitions

  • Arse Elektronika 2009, San Francisco, CA

Description

Description

Max/MSP patch used during presentation to show the biometrics of the presenter to the audience. The presenter wears devices that read pulse rate and brain activity, which is then displayed behind the presenter along with their slides. This gives the audience an intimate view of the presenter's internal state, regardless of how calm and collected they may seem outwardly.
